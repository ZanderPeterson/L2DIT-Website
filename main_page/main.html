What is the Hastings Youth Council?

The Hastings Youth Council is a group of 19 youth aged 15-21.
They host various youth focussed Events, represent youth in Council, manage Youth Grants, and more!
This year, they have elected Milly Petrie as Chair and Phaizon Parks as Deputy Chair.


How are Youth Councillors Selected?

Towards the end of every year, a form can be found online with various questions relating to Youth Council. To be accepted, an applicant must:
1. Be "15-21 years" of age
2. Either "live, work or attend school within the Hastings District"
If an applicant fits these criteria, then they may be shortlisted for an interview. If the interview is successful, they become a member of the Hastings Youth Council!
If someone was already a Hastings Youth Councillor, and would like to be apart of the Hastings Youth Council again, then they don't have to go through the interview proccess.


Who elects the Youth Councillors?

There are 3 individuals behind the process. Councillor Wendy Schollum (An elected individual), and Council Officers Renee Makea and Emma James, who are employees of the Hastings District Council and must remain politically unbiased.
