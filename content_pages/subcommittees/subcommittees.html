How Does Youth Council Represent Youth in Council?

Youth Councillors get opportunities to partake in various Subcommittees as non-voting members.
Between 1-3 youth may offer the youth perspective when decisions are being made in the various subcommittees.


But... What Even Are Subcommittees?

The Hastings District Council has various committees and subcommittees that make decisions relating to their respective area.
For example the Civic Development Subcommittee may make decisions surrounding road closures and funding allocations.
These committees and subcommittees are comprised mostly of Councillors, although Youth Councillors also tend to be apart of the meetings.
