What Are Youth Grants?

Youth Grants are an opportunity for youth to get funding.
This funding could be used to attend a sporting event, or start a business, purchace equipment, and more.
More information can be found here.


How Much Funding Can You Get Via Youth Grants

With Youth Grants, you can get up to $1000.
If you apply for more than $400, you may be asked to have an interview.


How Are Youth Grants Funded?

Youth Grants rely purely on fundraising organised by the Hastings Youth Council.


When Can I Apply?
